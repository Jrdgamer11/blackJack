# Welcome to Black Jack!
Black Jack is an open source project of the popular card game blackjack. You can download it here using the instructions under the section "How to download", or you can use the Jrdgamer11 Downloader (Coming soon!).

# How to play:
Playing Black Jack is very simple! The first thing you will notice is the amount of money you have, you will start with 5 dollars. Below that is the cards and your buttons. To play, simply choose your bet (It stacks!), then press start. Based on what cards you get you can stand or you can hit. Hitting will give you 1 extra card (max of five), and standing will lock your cards and run the dealer code.

# Did you win?
If you did, Congrats! Your money will be added to your account and the game will save. If you didn't, your money will be adjusted and saved.

# Run out of money?
Dont worry, there's a button below to reset it.

# How to download:
1. Put all downloaded files into a new folder and name it "bjRoot".
2. Click on "index.html" ONCE.
3. Right click on it then select "show more options" >> "Send to" >> "Desktop".
4. Go to your desktop and click on the title of the shortcut
5. Rename it to "BlackJack" (or whatever you want)
6. Your done!

# Optional instructions:
Follow these instructions to make your version look better.
1. Select the shortcut created in the last steps on your desktop
2. Select "Properties" >> "Change icon" >> "Browse"
3. Find your "bjRoot" folder >> "favicon.ico"
4. Done!
